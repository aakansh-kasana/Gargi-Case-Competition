Cheap umbrellas break quickly and become landfill waste

How ReRain Is Made

From discarded plastic bottles to a premium sustainable umbrella — every step is designed for minimal environmental impact.

Step 1

Collect & Recycle

Plastic bottles are collected and recycled

Step 2

Create Fabric

Bottles are converted into recycled rPET fabric

Step 3

Apply Coating

Fabric receives a self-healing bio-polymer coating

Step 4

Assemble

Modular umbrella is assembled using recyclable materials
